Physics

The study of how this universe works. It's easy for new students of the subject to get bogged down by symbols and equations. By drawing the right pictures, analyzing units, and thinking about reasonable outcomes, we will shift the focus from the equations to the physical phenomena they are meant to describe.

AP Physics, Introductory Physics, Astronomy, Electricity and Magnetism

Chemistry

High school chemisty isn't all lighting things on fire and mixing substances in beakers. It also requires you to internalize the atomic perspective. That is, it forces you to look at the world as an unfathomably large number of incredibly tiny objects and their interactions with one another. I have helped many students gain this perspective and given them all the tools they need to succeed in this class.

SAT/ACT

An entrance requirement for many colleges and universities. I help students achieve their dream score by creating a study plan, drilling practice problems, and teaching them tricks to optimize time and guessing. Using these strategies, many of my students far surpass their own expectations
